About me

A 20-year-old French student in my second year of Graphic Design at the Esad Amiens, I spent most of my teenage years in West Africa (Côte d’Ivoire and Senegal). Very open to people and cultures, I’m always looking for new experiences and encounters,new ways of learning and creating. I’m particularly interested in illustration and publishing, and I’d like to complete my studies with a Master’s degree and move towards careers in these two fields.
