Project

La Disparition

Creation of a series of images inspired by the foreword to the book "La Disparition" by Georges Perec. Two of these images were then used to propose two covers for Perec's book.

One of this project’s objectives was to learn how to handle IA as a graphic designer by using it to create half the images.
