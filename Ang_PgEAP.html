Project

Artist box on Edgar Allan Poe

Project to promote the work of the American writer Edgar Allan Poe and to expand his image with the general public who knows him only as a morbid and strange writer. The different objects in this box, ranging from publishing to linocut, through typographical creation, allow a first approach to his bibliography and show the extent of his influence on our culture.

Description

In order of appearance:

  • Poster of the box, size 60x80 cm
  • A set of three typographic posters, size 35x50 cm, containing quotes from Edgar Allan Poe, and the typographical alphabet created for this project
  • Leporello illustrating the poem "The Raven", 19x17 cm (closed), 12 pages
    Illustrations realised on Clip Studio Pro
  • Catalogue of works influenced by Edgar Allan Poe, A4 format (closed), 54 pages
  • Bookmarks en linogravure, 15x6 cm
  • Brochure on the cryptographic method of Edgar Allan Poe, format 12x12 cm (closed), 24x48cm
