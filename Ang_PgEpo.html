Project

Epologia

The Epologia project is based on the idea of creating a magazine to disseminate science to a wide audience. To facilitate the learning of readers and capture their attention, the magazine focuses on image, most of which were designed specifically for this project, as well as a predominant graphic vocabulary, stimulating and catchy.
The objective is to propose an object that the reader wants to browse and discover, both for its aesthetics and for the richness of its content.
