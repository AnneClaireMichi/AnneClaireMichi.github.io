Project

Ouroboros type specimen

A type specimen for the character Ouroboros, drawn by Ariel Martin Pérez in 2018 with the help of Helène Alix Mourrier, and her cover. The whole project was created to resemble a vinyl and its envelope and seeks to highlight the quality of the font’s titration character.
